Productive Loans

1. Business Loan

  • It can be availed with existing Quick Loan and or Promo Loan if the share Capital is higher than the sum of quick loan and promo loan.
  • Maximum Amount: PhP 500,000.00
  • Maximum Term: 12 Months

2. Special Promo Loans

  • An occasional loan product for special needs such as but not limited to increase business capital during Christmas Season or as the cooperative may deem necessary. Available only for common members and upon offering of the Manager.
  • Maximum Amount: PhP 100,000.00
  • Maximum Term: 6 Months

3. Livelihood Loan

  • Loan granted to individuals to help start or expand shall business or livelihood activities. Designed to support economic growth and poverty reduction by empowering people to become self support or generate income.
  • Maximum Amount: PhP 15,000.00
  • Maximum Term: 6 Months

4. Quick Loan - Productive

  • Equivalent to Share Capital and other deposits. Profitablility like open small business.
  • Maximum Amount: Based on Share Capital and other deposits.
  • Maximum Term: 12 Months

5. Collateralized Loan

  • It can be availed with an existing loan if it will be secured by Real Estate Mortgage.
  • 80% of approved value - subject to policy on single barrower
  • Issue a Post Dated Check (PDC)
  • Maximum Term: 5 Years (Subject to policy on % of long-term Loans)
